Our vision



To impact our community, by showing love and care and to see lives transformed by the power of Jesus Christ.


New Day Christian Church is a diverse congregation with multicultural roots and mixed generations.


We are a family orientated church who strive to show the love of Christ by commitment to community. We seek to help the community and to be relevant to needs of our community today.


We are a part of the The Foursquare Gospel Church (Australia) which is part of the global Foursquare Gospel Church, an international Pentecostal family of churches. Our focus is caring for people, both in Australia and internationally through expressing love and acceptance, inviting people to come into a knowledge of the Gospel and its forgiving grace.

Our values



PRESENCE

We believe God exists, that He is Good, and that His presence is to be encountered daily.


PEOPLE

We believe that every person is created by God and is inherently valuable in His sight.


POSITION

We live from a position of Kingdom assurance. We are saved by grace, forgiven, purposed

and destined to become the people God created us to be.


PROMISE

We believe The Word of God is powerful and transformative, and foundational to all we do.


PROPHECY We value a prophetic culture that shapes us and compels us to be the people we are becoming in Christ
